What is a Scrum?

Why Scrum is the vital framework in contemporary project management? Scrum is one of the most popular Agile methodologies. It is a flexible and adaptive, fast and iterative.

Using Scrum you can increase Return of Investment (ROI) in a early stages of project. Not only in software development but also in other fields Scrum helps to deliver high quality products in the shortest time.

The strength of Scrum lies in its team. Using Scrum team team becomes of cross-functional, self-organized and empowered. The Scrum team are the group of individuals which collaborate together to create the products or services.

Based on Agile manifesto Scrum consists six principles, five aspects and nineteen processes which are grouped into five phases.

According to Scrum principles teams divide their work into short, concentrated work cycles called Sprints. Sprint lasts two for four weeks.
